Nesse Piano Mystery

The Nesse Piano Mystery is an unsolved mystery concerning pianos with the brand name “Nesse” scattered around various rural locations in Juxta Sagittaria, with the first discoveries of the pianos being made in 691, 693, and 694. A total of 6 pianos have been found so far.

No real reasoning has been deduced from the discovery of the pianos, although some patterns have been observed:

  • The pianos are all of the same brand, Nesse, a musical instrument company that ran from 256 to 295.
  • The pianos are of various models from the company.
  • All pianos are in relatively remote and rural locations.
  • All pianos show little sign of use, although some have aged considerably upon discovery.
