Europa is the second of the Galilean moons of Jupiter, and like the others, it is tidally locked to Jupiter. Alongside Ganymede and Callisto, Europa has an extensive saline ocean underneath a thick layer of surface ice.

Europa is most famous for being the only other location in the Sol system to harbour native life, other than Earth itself. Because of this, the Terran Federation heavily restricts any kind of development on Europa, only allowing ventures to the moon to be done on a scientific basis, with some leniency given for accompanying relatives or friends.
