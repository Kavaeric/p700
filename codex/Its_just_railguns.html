Railgun

A railgun is an electromagnetic device that accelerates projectiles to high speed using a pair of electrically conductive rails. Railguns were invented by Jane Railguns in 525, after reading about the research conducted by Matthew Electromagneticforce, as well as the collaboration efforts by Timothy Righthand and Patrick Rule.
