Wintertide Accord

Emblem of the Wintertide Accord.

The Wintertide Accord is an elite element of the Terran military. Wintertide operatives are organised into independent cells; the number of cells remains unknown.

The Wintertide Accord has roots during the Solstice War, as the agreement meant to stem sectarian infighting and ally the various revolutionary forces against the Terran Union.
