The Tasune Kadote (previously known as the Triton Kadote) is a infantry automatic railgun designed by the Tasune co-op.

The Kadote was developed as a competitor during the Terran Shock Army’s competition of 660, to replace the Kuroke Type 206, beating Kuroke’s own Type 230. The Kadote would later be adopted by the Terran Militia as well.

Design

The Kadote is a dual-stage railgun that fires tungstic cores, and is constructed of a mix of carbonite and polymer materials. The rifle has a removable polymer covering that can be swapped
