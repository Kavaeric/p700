Ranger

Codex information
This page is a work in progress. Information about this topic may be incomplete.

Ranger emblem.

A ranger is a person specially trained to operate in extremely isolated locations for extended periods of time, including operating in hazardous environments. Rangers are members of the Ranger Union, an agency of the Terran Federation.

Rangers always work in pairs. A ranger unit is comprised of two rangers:

  • The aeranger, an aerosynth who can better travel long distances;
  • The teranger, a biped, who can better navigate in smaller scales, such as on the surface of a planet or in a structure.
